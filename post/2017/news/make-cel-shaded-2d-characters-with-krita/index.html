Make Cel Shaded 2d Characters With Krita

The second Krita game art tutorial series is out! Meet Pepper, Carrot, and cel shading. This course is based on the world of Pepper&Carrot, a webcomic under the CCBy 4.0 licence by David Revoy.

What you will learn

  1. How to create game characters with a cartoon shaded art style
  2. Lighting and shading tricks with Krita

This is a short, yet a rich series. It comes with 14 video tutorials, plus a fully commented art time-lapse.

Pre-requisites

  1. If you have never used Krita before or don’t feel comfortable with it, check out my 6 hours long free Krita course.
  2. The pace of the course is like the tutorials you will find on the GDquest channel, you can check them out first and see if they work for you before you buy.
